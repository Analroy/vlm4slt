# vlm4slt — Can Small Vision–Language Models Perform Sign Language Translation?

[![ACL 2026](https://img.shields.io/badge/ACL-2026%20Findings-blue)](https://aclanthology.org/)

**Anal Roy Chowdhury and Debarshi Kumar Sanyal**
Indian Association for the Cultivation of Science (IACS), Kolkata, India

---

Official code for the ACL 2026 Findings paper. We present the first systematic evaluation of small-scale Vision–Language Models (≤3B parameters) — **SmolVLM2-2.2B**, **Qwen2.5-VL-3B**, and **InternVL3-2B** — for Sign Language Translation across German (DGS), American (ASL), and Indian (ISL) sign languages. Models are fine-tuned with parameter-efficient **LoRA** on four benchmarks: PHOENIX-2014T, How2Sign, OpenASL, and iSign.

We also introduce two new SLT evaluation metrics: **Translation Soft Recall (TSR)** and **Pseudo-Gloss Entity Recall (PGER)**.
